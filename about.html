About the DJP Invitational Golf Tournament

Our Mission

The DJP Invitational is an annual golf trip that brings together friends and family for a weekend of fun, competition, and camaraderie. Along side having fun, our mission is to raise money and support for organizations and individuals in need.

Founding and History

In 2011 Nicholas Poulakos created the DJP Invitational Golf Tournament in honor of his father Dennis James Poulakos. The annual event has beem spread by word of mouth to friends and family over the years bringing to 2024 tournament to be the first with a full 64 player roster. Every year the tournament is used as a fundraiser to a variety of causes, check out the donations page for more information.
